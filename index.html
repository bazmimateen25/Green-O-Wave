About Green O Wave

Pioneering the Electric Revolution

Green O Wave is a premier EV expo dedicated to showcasing the latest innovations in electric vehicle technology. Founded by Afroz Abedin, we are committed to promoting sustainable transportation solutions.

Located in Muzaffarpur, Bihar, we bring together industry leaders, innovators, and enthusiasts to celebrate the electric vehicle revolution.
